About Galaxy Upcycling

Galaxy Upcycling provides an environmentally responsible way for old Galaxy mobile devices to breathe new life, providing limitless possiblities and extended value for devices taht might otherwise be forgotten in desk drawers or discarded.

Galaxy Upcycling is not only sustainable by extending the useful life of old phones, but through innovation and open source, it also builds a community for social welfare.
